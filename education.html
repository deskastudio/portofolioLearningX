SDN SERDANG WETAN

When I attended SDN Serdang Wetan, I actively participated in extracurricular Scout activities and attended English courses. While attending Dirghantara Junior High School, I remained active in Scouting and Student Council activities. I even served as Vice Chairman of the student council. I also often made achievements in Scouting activities. In addition, I had a close relationship with the teachers at the junior high school, as they were very friendly and pleasant.

SMAN 17 KAB. TANGERANG

At SMA Negeri 17 Tangerang Regency, I was active in Class Representative Council (MPK) activities and served as Class President for two years. Although the last year of school was conducted online due to the COVID-19 pandemic, I was still active in the activities organized at the school.
